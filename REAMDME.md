# 🐻 Habit Jar

A minimal habit tracker to build and maintain daily streaks.

Add habits, mark them complete each day, and watch your 7-day history at a glance — with streak counts and a daily completion rate.

## Storage

All data is saved locally in your browser via `localStorage` under the key `habit-tracker-data`. Nothing is sent to any server. Clearing your browser data will reset your habits.
